Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz AG produces consumer luxury vehicles and light commercial vehicles badged as Mercedes-Benz. From November 2019 onwards, Mercedes-Benz-badged heavy commercial vehicles (trucks and buses) are managed by Daimler Truck, a former part of the Mercedes-Benz Group turned into an independent company in late 2021. In 2018, Mercedes-Benz was the largest brand of premium vehicles in the world, having sold 2.31 million passenger cars.[8]

company history

The revenue of Mercedes-Benz Group increased by over 2.1 percent in the 2023 fiscal year. As of 2023, the company registered revenue of around 153.2 billion euros, against around 150 billion euros in 2022. The Mercedes-Benz Group is a German multinational automotive corporation formerly named Daimler AG, which split from Daimler Truck in December 2021.
